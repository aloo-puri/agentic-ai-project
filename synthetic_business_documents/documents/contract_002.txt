RECITALS
The Manufacturer produces dietary supplements, protein powders, and wellness products. The Distributor operates retail distribution network across Maharashtra and Gujarat. Both parties wish to establish distribution arrangement on mutually beneficial terms.

AGREEMENT TERMS

Article 1 - Appointment
Manufacturer appoints Distributor as non-exclusive distributor for the Products in the Territory comprising Maharashtra and Gujarat states.

Article 2 - Products and Pricing
Products covered include all items listed in Annexure A. Manufacturer shall provide price list updated quarterly. Distributor receives trade discount of 28% on MRP.

Article 3 - Minimum Purchase Commitment
Distributor commits to minimum quarterly purchases of INR 75,00,000. Failure to meet commitment for two consecutive quarters permits Manufacturer to terminate agreement.

Article 4 - Marketing Support  
Manufacturer provides marketing collateral, product samples, and contributes 2% of quarterly purchases toward local marketing activities undertaken by Distributor.

Article 5 - Duration and Renewal
Initial term of 3 years commencing 1 January 2026. Automatically renews for successive 1-year periods unless either party provides 90 days written termination notice.

Article 6 - Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by laws of India. Disputes subject to arbitration in Mumbai under Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996.
