FRANCHISE GRANT

Franchisor grants Franchisee right to establish and operate one Coffee Culture café at commercial property in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad (exact location subject to Franchisor approval).

Territory: Exclusive rights within 2 km radius of approved location.

FEES AND ROYALTIES

Initial franchise fee: INR 15,00,000 (non-refundable)
Setup and training costs: INR 12,00,000
Interior and equipment package: INR 25,00,000  

Ongoing monthly royalty: 6% of gross sales
Marketing contribution: 2% of gross sales for national marketing fund

TERM
Initial franchise term of 5 years commencing from café opening date. Franchisee has option to renew for additional 5 years subject to performance criteria and payment of renewal fee.

TRAINING AND SUPPORT
Franchisor provides 2 weeks training for Franchisee and staff covering operations, customer service, and quality standards. Ongoing support includes marketing materials, menu updates, and operational guidance.

STANDARDS AND OPERATIONS  
Franchisee must maintain quality standards, use approved suppliers, follow pricing guidelines, and operate during minimum hours specified in Operations Manual. Mystery shopper audits conducted quarterly.

TERMINATION
Franchisor may terminate for: failure to pay fees, breach of quality standards, unauthorized menu changes, or sale without approval. Franchisee may terminate with 6 months notice after completing minimum 3 years.

NON-COMPETE
Franchisee shall not operate competing café business during franchise term and 2 years post-termination within 5 km of franchise location.
