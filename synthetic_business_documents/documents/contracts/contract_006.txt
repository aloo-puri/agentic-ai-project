MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Ref: MOU/JV/2026/AB-CD
Date: 28 January 2026

Background and Intent

This document captures the understanding between Alpha Constructions Limited (Alpha) and BuildTech Engineering Pvt Ltd (BuildTech) regarding proposed joint venture for executing infrastructure projects in Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh.

Alpha brings 25 years construction experience, equipment fleet worth Rs 85 crores, and existing contracts portfolio. BuildTech contributes project management expertise, client relationships with state PWD departments, and working capital availability.

Proposed Structure

Parties intend forming special purpose vehicle (SPV) as 50-50 joint venture company. Initial capitalization Rs 20 crores with equal contribution. SPV will bid for and execute road construction, bridge projects, and water supply infrastructure.

Financial Framework  

Profit and loss sharing proportionate to equity holding. Each party may provide loans to SPV at 12% annual interest if additional funding required. Distributions subject to maintaining minimum working capital of Rs 5 crores.

Operational Governance

Board comprises 4 directors - 2 nominees each party. CEO appointed by Alpha, CFO by BuildTech. Decisions requiring unanimous board approval include: capital expenditure exceeding Rs 2 crores, asset acquisitions, entering contracts over Rs 10 crores, creating borrowings beyond approved limits.

Duration and Exit

MOU valid 90 days for completing due diligence and definitive agreements. Post-JV formation, minimum lock-in 5 years. Either party may exit thereafter through sale to other party at fair value determined by independent valuer.

Exclusivity

During MOU validity, neither party shall discuss similar arrangements with third parties for Karnataka/Andhra Pradesh region without other party's written consent.

This MOU represents good faith intent and not legally binding obligation except confidentiality and exclusivity provisions.
